Mia Tong


Officer Position: Social Manager
Hometown: Tokyo, Japan
Years in Cal Taiko/Playing Taiko: 2/2
Year in School: Third Year

Biography: While living in Japan as a young girl, Mia knew Taiko as "that cool thing" that she always wanted the chance to play. Over ten years later, she finally found her chance to do so with Cal Raijin Taiko, and is loving every second of it! She loves feeling the energy that is unlike any other when playing Taiko. Other than Taiko, she enjoys singing, playing the Oud (an Arabic instrument), exploring bizarre and unheard of places with friends, and reading.
