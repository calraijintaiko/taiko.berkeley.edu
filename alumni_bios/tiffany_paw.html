Tiffany Paw

Officer Position: Administrative Director
Hometown: San Bruno, CA
Years in Cal Taiko/Playing Taiko: 4/4
Year in School: Fourth Year
Major: Integrative Biology
Minor: Chinese

Biography: In 2006, Tiffany got her first taste of taiko after watching San Francisco Taiko Dojo’s concert with her high school’s Japanese cultural club, and was inspired to learn taiko for herself. The opportunity finally presented itself when Tiffany witnessed Cal Raijin Taiko’s 2008 Calapalooza performance, and she immediately signed up for tryouts. Four years later, taiko has become a newfound passion as well as a dependable sanctuary away from the daily stress of being a Berkeley student. Outside of taiko, you’ll find Tiffany studying up on zoology, taking care of tuco tucos, practicing her Mandarin and Japanese, taking photos, or playing video games. Finally, being born and raised in the Bay Area, Tiffany can proudly say, “I hella love taiko!”
